BIRYANI

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish layered with spiced meat or vegetables. It's a celebration dish in many South Asian cultures and known for its rich taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Basic Seasoning
    • oil
    • salt
    • water
  • Herbs
    • Fresh Mint
    • cilantro
  • 1/2 lb chicken or vegetables
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt

Instructions

  1. Cook rice until 80% done and set aside.
  2. In a pot, saut√© onions in oil until golden.
  3. Add meat/veggies, yogurt, masala, salt, and cook until done.
  4. Layer rice over the meat, sprinkle herbs, and cover.
  5. Cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Mix gently before serving.
