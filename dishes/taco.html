TACO

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican street food made with soft tortillas filled with flavorful meats and fresh toppings. They're quick to prepare and fun to customize.

Ingredients

  • Small corn or flour tortillas
  • cooked meat
    • beef
    • chicken
    • pork
  • Chopped onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Salsa
  • wedges

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas in a pan.
  2. Fill each tortilla with cooked meat.
  3. with chopped onion and cilantro.
  4. salsa and a squeeze of lime.
  5. Serve immediately.
